WWE Hell IN A Cell (Raw)

Results

TJ PERKINS VS. BRIAN KENDRICK

WWE Cruiserweight Championship

Results: Kendrick faked a knee injury to fool the referee and Perkins. He then headbutted Perkins and made him tap to the Captain's Hook. New champion!

ROMAN REIGNS (C) VS. RUSEV

Hell in a Cell for the United States Championship

Results: Rusev put Reigns in the Accolade with a chain while on top of steel steps. Reigns stood up and slammed Rusev onto the steps. As Rusev got back to his feet, Reigns hit the Spear and pinned him to retain.

BAYLEY VS. DANA BROOKE

Results: Dana attacked Bayley's injured arm, but stumbled into Belly-to-Bayley and ate the pin.

BIG CASS AND ENZO AMORE VS. THE CLUB

Results: After removing Cass from the ring, Anderson and Gallows took apart Enzo and finished him off with the Magic Killer.

SETH ROLLINS VS. KEVIN OWENS (C)

Hell in a Cell for the WWE Universal Championship

Results: By accidentally-on-purpose blinding the ref with a fire extinguisher, Owens was able to get the door opened, allowing Chris Jericho in. Rollins put Owens through two tables and hit a frog splash, but Jericho pulled the referee out of the ring. Rollins tried to fight off the two and even survived a Pop-Up Powerbomb, but then Owens and Jericho sat up two chairs and had Owens powerbomb Rollins through them. After the match, Jericho beat up Rollins some more.

NEW DAY (C) VS. CESARO AND SHEAMUS

WWE Tag Team Championship

Results: New Day chose Big E and Xavier Woods to represent them. At the end of the match, Cesaro had Xavier in the Sharpshooter. Sheamus had a scuffle with Big E, ending with Sheamus hitting him with Francesca II. Kofi hit Trouble in Paradise on Sheamus, the ref saw it, and the challengers won by disqualification.

SASHA BANKS (C) VS. CHARLOTTE

Hell in a Cell for the WWE Women's Championship (Raw)

Results: After a very lengthy battle with a false start, Sasha prepared to powerbomb Charlotte through a table, but her back gave out. Charlotte continually slammed Sasha into the table until pinning her. Charlotte wins the championship.
