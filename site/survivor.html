WWE Survivor Series

Results

NIA JAX, SASHA BANKS, CHARLOTTE, BAYLEY, AND ALICIA FOX
VS. CARMELLA, BECKY LYNCH, NIKKI BELLA, NATALYA, ALEXA BLISS, AND NAOMI


Results: Nikki was found backstage, too hurt to compete. Natalya took her spot.

Alicia Fox took down Carmella early on. 5-4 for Raw.

Alexa Bliss splashed onto Alicia Fox from the top rope, tying it at 4-4.

Nia Jax threw Naomi off the apron and onto the floor. Naomi was too hurt to make it in for the ten count. 4-3.

Natalya surprised Sasha with a roll-up, bringing SmackDown up 3-3.

Charlotte knocked out Natalya with a rising knee. 3-2 for Raw.

Nia Jax tapped out to the Disarmer. Afterwards, she beat down Becky. 2-2.

Charlotte took advantage and worked on Alexa, pinning her and making it 2-1.

The numbers were too much and Bayley pinned Becky after nailiny the Bayley to Belly. Charlotte and Bayley were the survivors of Team Raw. Afterwards, Charlotte beat down Bayley.

MIZ VS. SAMI ZAYN

Intercontinental Championship

Rematch: Sami Zayn had the Miz in the Figure Four. Maryse rang the bell, making Zayn believe he won the match. As the ref tried to retain order, Miz rolled up Zayn and grabbed the tights. Miz retains and Raw keeps the Intercontinental Championship.

SHINING STARS, SHEAMUS, CESARO, THE CLUB, BIG CASS, ENZO,
AND THE NEW DAY VS. BREEZANGO, AMERICAN ALPHA,
THE USOS, BEAUTY AND THE MANBEAST, AND THE HYPE BROS


Tag-Team Elimination Match

Results: Fandango started handing out fashion violation tickets. Big E grabbed him and delivered the Midnight Hour. 10-8.

Jimmy Uso immediately superkicked Kofi Kingston and pinned him. New Day is out! 8-8.

The Club dropped Zack Ryder with the Magic Killer. Raw's got the advantage at 8-6.

American Alpha delivered a Doomsday Device bulldog to one of the Shining Stars. 6-6.

American Alpha were doing great in a huge melee, but then they got taken down by the Club, who finished Jason Jordan with the Magic Killer. 6-4.

Rhyno Gored through Gallows and the Club is out. We're back to a tie, 4-4.

Enzo and Cass take out Rhyno and Slater with a Big Boot and Air Enzo. 4-2.

Jey Uso splashed onto Enzo and brought it to 2-2.

Cesaro put one Uso in the Sharpshooter. The other Uso was going to break it up, but got intercepted with the Brogue Kick. One tap out later and Sheamus and Cesaro won the match for Raw. Sheamus offered to shake Cesaro's hand, but he pulled it away at the last second.

Brian Kendrick vs Kalisto

Cruiserweight Championship

Results: Baron Corbin did a run-in and attacked both of them. He took down Kendrick first, meaning Kendrock won via DQ.

BRAUN STROWMAN, SETH ROLLINS, CHRIS JERICHO, ROMAN REIGNS,
AND KEVIN OWENS VS. AJ STYLES, DEAN AMBROSE,
SHANE MCMAHON, BRAY WYATT, AND RANDY ORTON


Tag-Team Elimination Match

Results:

After AJ Styles and Dean Ambrose started fighting each other, Braun Strowman took advantage by crushing Ambrose with a running powerslam. Raw in the lead with 5-4.

Randy Orton RKO'd Braun Strowman into a table. Then he and Wyatt set Braun up and allowed Shane to jump off the top and elbow Braun through it. Braun got up to beat the count, but didn't make it. Turns out, James Ellsworth was under the ring, grabbing him by the leg. Braun then annihilated Ellsworth and through him through a table near the ramp. 4-4.

As AJ Styles worked on Jericho, Owens came in and wailed on Styles with Jericho's list. Owens was disqualified. 4-3.

Jericho was distraught over the pages of the list littering the ring. As he tried to gather them all, he turned around into an RKO. 4-2.

Shane McMahon went for Coast to Coast and got hit with a Spear from Roman. The ref stopped counting at 2, checked on Shane, and called for him to be eliminated. Looks like something might have gone wrong. 3-2.

Ambrose returned to attack Styles. Security dragged him off, but Roman and Rollins saved him. The three of them did a Shield Triple Powerbomb to Styles through a table. Rollins rolled Styles into the ring and pinned him. 2-2.

Rollins went off the top for a splash on Wyatt, but Orton caught him with an RKO. 2-1.

Orton shoved Wyatt out of the way and ate a Spear. Wyatt grabbed Roman and ended him with Sister Abigail. Team SmackDown wins!

BROCK LESNAR VS. BILL GOLDBERG

Results: Goldberg shoved Lesnar, hit two Spears, the Jackhammer, and pin. Total squash over in just over a minute.
